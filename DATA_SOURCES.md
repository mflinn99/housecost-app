# Data Sources (Manchester + Salford)

This product is intentionally scoped to Manchester and Salford.

## Benchmarks used

- **Area pricing context**: UK House Price Index style district-level benchmarks (Manchester, Salford) used as local baseline anchors.
- **Sold-price context**: Local sold-price reference values for decision positioning and negotiation context.
- **Affordability assumptions**: Mortgage-rate and payment-share assumptions aligned to public UK affordability framing.

## How we use them

- Area baselines support:
  - local price position
  - trend direction
  - buyer/seller leverage cues
- Mortgage assumptions support:
  - monthly ownership cost estimates
  - affordability pressure signal
- Decision outputs are rule-based and transparent (no ML claims).

## Important trust note

These are **decision-support benchmarks**, not live valuations or financial advice. They are designed to be refreshable as newer public data is ingested.
