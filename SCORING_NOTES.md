# Scoring & Ranking Logic

## Badges (deterministic)

- **Lowest Monthly Cost** — Property with minimum total estimated monthly cost
- **Lowest Upfront Cost** — Property with minimum deposit + upfront
- **Best Transport Access** — Shortest walking time to nearest bus/tram/train
- **Best Lifestyle Density** — Highest amenities (bars + restaurants + cafes)
- **Highest Vibe Score** — Highest composite Vibe Score
- **Strongest Discount Potential** — Highest negotiation likelihood
- **Best Overall Value** — TBD: combine cost, transport, lifestyle

## Vibe Score factors

- Amenities density (bars, restaurants, cafes)
- Supermarket convenience
- Gym access
- Transport convenience
- Quietness vs bustle balance
- Parking practicality
- Demographics (directional)
